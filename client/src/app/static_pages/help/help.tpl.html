  • How to use Statium

    1. To enter your data, click on the Data menu. You can also copy and paste data from an Excel spreadsheet. The first column of data should contain the between-subject factor, and the rest of the columns can each contain a measurement of the within-subject factor.
    2. To enter contrasts, click on the Analysis menu, and in the Contrast panel enter the contrasts by clicking the New Contrast button.
    3. To analyze the data, click on the Analysis button in the Contrast panel and the analysis will appear below the Contrast panel. If the data or contrasts change, you will need to repeat this step to get a new analysis.
    4. You can click on the Summary menu at any stage to see a summary of the data.
